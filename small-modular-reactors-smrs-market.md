<p><p>Small Modular Reactors (SMRs) are compact nuclear reactors designed for scalability and enhanced safety. They are categorized into two primary market types: thermal-neutron reactors, which utilize low-energy thermal neutrons for fission and are commonly used in traditional nuclear power, and fast-neutron reactors, which rely on high-energy neutrons to sustain fission reactions and can efficiently utilize a wider range of nuclear fuels. SMRs offer flexible deployment options and potential for reduced costs and operational risks in the nuclear energy sector.</p></p>
