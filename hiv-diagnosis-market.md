<p><p>The HIV diagnosis market encompasses several key test types. Antibody tests detect the presence of antibodies produced in response to HIV, offering a quick screening method. Viral load tests measure the amount of virus in the bloodstream, essential for monitoring disease progression. CD4 tests assess immune system health by counting CD4 T cells. Early infant diagnosis tests identify HIV in infants, crucial for timely treatment. Lastly, tests for viral identification differentiate HIV types, ensuring accurate treatment and management strategies.</p></p>
