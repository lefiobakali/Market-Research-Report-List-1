<p><p>CNC automatic lathes are categorized primarily into horizontal and vertical types, each serving distinct manufacturing needs. Horizontal lathes are designed for greater stability and are typical for longer, heavier workpieces, allowing efficient continuous cutting operations. In contrast, vertical lathes are ideal for machining larger, workpieces with substantial height, providing enhanced accessibility and gravity assistance during the cutting process. Both types enable precision machining, automated control, and improved productivity, catering to diverse industries like automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
