<p><strong>The Shot Blasters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hanger Type</li><li>Tumblast Machine</li><li>Continuous Through-feed</li><li>Rotary Table</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Shot blasters are categorized into several types based on their operational design and application. Hanger type shot blasters are suspended systems ideal for large components. Tumblast machines handle smaller parts in a rotating drum, ensuring thorough cleaning. Continuous through-feed blasters move items along a conveyor for high-speed processing. Rotary table blasters use a circular table to hold parts while being blasted. Other types may include specialized systems tailored for unique applications, enhancing efficiency and versatility in surface preparation.</p></p>
