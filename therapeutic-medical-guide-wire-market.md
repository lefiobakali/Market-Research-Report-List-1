<p><ul><li>Solid Guide Wire</li><li>Wrapped Guide Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The therapeutic medical guide wire market comprises two primary types: solid guide wires and wrapped guide wires. Solid guide wires are rigid and provide stability, making them ideal for navigating through complex vascular systems. In contrast, wrapped guide wires feature an outer layer that enhances flexibility and allows smoother navigation, reducing trauma to surrounding tissues. Both types are crucial in various minimally invasive procedures, supporting the placement of catheters and other devices with precision and safety.</p></p>
