ZW Type pumps are designed for transferring clean water and slightly contaminated liquids, often used for irrigation and drainage in agriculture. In contrast, QW Type pumps are suitable for handling sewage and wastewater, making them ideal for agricultural applications where waste management is crucial. Both types enhance efficiency and adaptability in farming operations, addressing diverse fluid handling needs.</p></p>
