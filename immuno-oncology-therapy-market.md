<p><p>Immuno-oncology therapy market encompasses various types targeting cancer treatment through immune system modulation. Monoclonal antibodies are lab-engineered proteins that specifically target cancer cells, enhancing immune response. Immune checkpoint inhibitors block proteins that prevent immune cells from attacking tumors, rejuvenating the immune response. Immune system modulators stimulate overall immune activity to improve cancer defense. Cancer vaccines aim to prime the immune system against specific cancer antigens, enabling targeted destruction of tumor cells and promoting long-term immunity.</p></p>
