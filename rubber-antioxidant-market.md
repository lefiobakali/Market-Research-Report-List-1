<p><strong>The Rubber Antioxidant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PPDs</li><li>RD (TMQ)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rubber antioxidant market is primarily categorized into three types: PPDs (Paraphenylenediamines), RD (TMQ) (Rubber Decelerators or 4-Methylthio-2-methylphenol), and others. PPDs are widely used for their effectiveness in enhancing the aging resistance of rubber, especially in tires. RD (TMQ) serves as a stabilizer and antioxidant, protecting rubber from oxidation. Other types include various antioxidants that cater to specific needs and applications, contributing to improved performance, durability, and longevity of rubber products across industries.</p></p>
