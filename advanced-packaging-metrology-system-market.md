<p><p>The Advanced Packaging Metrology System market includes several key types, primarily Optical Based Packaging Metrology Systems and Infrared Packaging Metrology Systems. Optical-based systems utilize light to measure and assess the dimensions and alignment of packages with high precision, making them essential for fine-scale applications. In contrast, Infrared Packaging Metrology Systems leverage thermal imaging to evaluate package integrity and detect defects, particularly in semiconductor packaging. Both technologies are crucial for ensuring quality and performance in advanced packaging processes.</p></p>
