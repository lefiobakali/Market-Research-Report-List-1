<p><strong>The Cell Sorting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluorescent Activated Cell Sorting</li><li>Magnetic-activated Cell Sorting</li><li>MEMS-Microfluidics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cell sorting technologies are essential for isolating specific cell types for research and clinical applications. Fluorescent Activated Cell Sorting (FACS) uses fluorescent markers to identify and separate cells based on their characteristics, enabling precise analysis. Magnetic-Activated Cell Sorting (MACS) employs magnetic nanoparticles to selectively capture target cells from a mixture. MEMS-Microfluidics combines miniaturized systems and fluid control, allowing for high-throughput sorting and analysis of cells. Together, these methods enhance cellular research, diagnostics, and therapeutic developments.</p></p>
