<p><strong>The White Box Server Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rack-mount Server</li><li>Blade Server</li><li>Whole Cabinet Server</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The White Box Server market consists of three main types: Rack-mount Servers, Blade Servers, and Whole Cabinet Servers. Rack-mount Servers optimize space in data centers with standardized dimensions, allowing for efficient stacking and cooling. Blade Servers offer a compact design by housing multiple server blades in a single chassis, maximizing resource sharing and minimizing power consumption. Whole Cabinet Servers, on the other hand, are self-contained units that provide extensive capacity and scalability for large-scale computing needs, catering to enterprise-level applications.</p></p>
