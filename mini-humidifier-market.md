<p><ul><li>Steam Humidifier</li><li>Water Spray Humidifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mini humidifier market typically consists of two main types: steam humidifiers and water spray humidifiers. Steam humidifiers generate warm mist by boiling water, effectively increasing humidity and providing a soothing environment, particularly beneficial in dry climates. On the other hand, water spray humidifiers utilize cool mist technology, dispersing tiny water droplets into the air for moisture. While steam humidifiers are often preferred for therapeutic purposes, water spray models are favored for their ease of use and energy efficiency.</p></p>
