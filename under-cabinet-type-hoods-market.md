<p><ul><li>Axial Flow Hood</li><li>Centrifugal Hood</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Under cabinet type hoods are designed for efficient ventilation in kitchens, available in two primary market types: axial flow hoods and centrifugal hoods. Axial flow hoods utilize a fan to move air directly through the duct, delivering quick ventilation and are ideal for smaller spaces. Centrifugal hoods, on the other hand, use a fan to create a high-pressure airflow, providing stronger suction and effectively removing smoke and odors, making them suitable for larger or more demanding cooking environments.</p></p>
