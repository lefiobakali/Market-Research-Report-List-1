<p><p>Light Guide Plates (LGPs) are essential components in Liquid Crystal Display Televisions (LCTVs), serving to distribute light evenly across the screen. The market is primarily divided into two types: print LGPs and print-less LGPs. Print LGPs utilize precise surface patterns to control light diffusion, enhancing brightness and uniformity. In contrast, print-less LGPs rely on advanced manufacturing techniques to achieve similar effects without printed designs, often resulting in thinner, lighter plates. Both types aim to improve display quality while optimizing production efficiency.</p></p>
