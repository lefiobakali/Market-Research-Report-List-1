<p><ul><li>Soft Magnetic Material</li><li>Permanent Magnetic Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetic materials market is primarily divided into soft magnetic materials and permanent magnetic materials. Soft magnetic materials, such as silicon steel and ferrites, are characterized by their easy magnetization and de-magnetization, making them ideal for applications like transformers and inductors. In contrast, permanent magnetic materials, including neodymium-iron-boron and samarium-cobalt, retain their magnetization and are used in motors, sensors, and data storage devices. Each type serves distinct functionalities in various industries, driving technological advancements and innovation.</p></p>
