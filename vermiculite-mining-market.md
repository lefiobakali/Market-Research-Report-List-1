<p><strong>The Vermiculite Mining Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crude Vermiculite</li><li>Exfoliated Vermiculite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vermiculite mining market is primarily categorized into two types: crude vermiculite and exfoliated vermiculite. Crude vermiculite refers to the raw, unprocessed mineral extracted from mines, which is primarily used in construction and insulation materials. Exfoliated vermiculite, on the other hand, is produced by heating crude vermiculite, causing it to expand and form a lightweight, fire-resistant material. This processed form is widely used in horticulture, packaging, and as a filler in various industrial applications, due to its superior properties.</p></p>
