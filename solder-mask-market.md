<p><p>The solder mask market includes various types based on application and formulation. Top and bottom side masks protect PCB surfaces during soldering, while epoxy liquid solder masks offer durability and chemical resistance. Liquid photoimageable solder masks provide precise patterning for fine-detail applications, utilizing UV light for exposure. Dry film photoimageable solder masks offer easy application and good resolving power, making them ideal for complex designs. Each type caters to specific printing and protective needs within the electronics manufacturing industry.</p></p>
