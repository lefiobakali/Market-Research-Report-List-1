<p><p>The vacuum suction device market comprises various types, notably steam jet pumps and dry vacuum pumps. Steam jet pumps utilize high-pressure steam to create a vacuum by propelling steam through nozzles, making them efficient for processes requiring high throughput, particularly in chemical and industrial applications. In contrast, dry vacuum pumps operate without fluids, providing reliable and maintenance-friendly vacuum solutions for applications in pharmaceuticals, food processing, and semiconductor manufacturing, offering cleaner environments free from contamination.</p></p>
