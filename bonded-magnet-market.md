<p><ul><li>Plastic Injected Magnets</li><li>Plastic Pressed Magnets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bonded magnet market encompasses two primary types: plastic injected magnets and plastic pressed magnets. Plastic injected magnets are produced by injecting molten materials into molds, allowing for intricate shapes and high precision, making them suitable for complex applications. In contrast, plastic pressed magnets are manufactured by compressing a mixture of magnet powders and binder materials, offering versatility in production and cost-effective solutions. Both types serve various industries, including automotive, electronics, and consumer goods, driving innovation and performance.</p></p>
