<p><p>An Auto Transformer Rectifier Unit (ATRU) is a power conversion system used in railway and industrial applications to efficiently convert alternating current (AC) into direct current (DC). ATRUs come in various configurations, defined by the number of pulses they generate. The 6 Pulse type is commonly used for lower power applications, while the 12 Pulse and 18 Pulse configurations provide higher efficiency and reduced harmonic distortion, making them suitable for demanding environments. Each type caters to specific load requirements and operational contexts.</p></p>
