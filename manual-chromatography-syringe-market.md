<p><p>The manual chromatography syringe market is categorized into two main types: fixed and removable syringes. Fixed syringes have a non-detachable needle, offering a consistent flow and reducing the risk of contamination, making them ideal for precise applications. Removable syringes, on the other hand, feature interchangeable needles, allowing for versatility in experiments and easier maintenance. Both types cater to diverse chromatographic needs in laboratories, providing options for different sample types and user preferences in the separation processes.</p></p>
