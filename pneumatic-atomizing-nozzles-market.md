<p><ul><li>Flat Spray Nozzles</li><li>Full Cone Nozzles</li><li>Hollow Cone Nozzles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic atomizing nozzles market comprises three main types: flat spray nozzles, full cone nozzles, and hollow cone nozzles. Flat spray nozzles produce a narrow, flat spray pattern ideal for coating surfaces evenly. Full cone nozzles generate a uniform spray pattern that covers a circular area, suitable for applications requiring thorough liquid distribution. Hollow cone nozzles create a ring-shaped spray, effective for mixing applications and cooling processes. These nozzle types cater to diverse industries, including agriculture, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
