<p><p>The pupilometer market is segmented into monocular and binocular types. Monocular pupilometers measure the size and parameters of one eye at a time, making them portable and often preferred in field settings. Conversely, binocular pupilometers simultaneously measure both eyes, providing a comprehensive view of ocular function and enhancing accuracy in assessments. These tools are essential for various applications, including ophthalmology, optometry, and research, facilitating the study of visual perception and diagnosing ocular conditions more effectively.</p></p>
