<p><p>Rotary sealers are specialized machines used in packaging to create airtight seals on products. The market can be divided into two main types: horizontal rotary sealers and vertical rotary sealers. Horizontal rotary sealers operate with products fed in a horizontal position, ideal for items like pouches and trays. In contrast, vertical rotary sealers accommodate products in an upright position, suited for bags and stand-up pouches. Both types enhance efficiency and speed in packaging processes across various industries.</p></p>
