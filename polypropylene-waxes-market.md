<p><p>The polypropylene wax market is divided into two main types: normal polypropylene (PP) wax and modified PP wax. Normal PP wax is primarily used for applications like coatings, adhesives, and plastics due to its excellent thermal stability and low viscosity. In contrast, modified PP wax is engineered to have enhanced properties, such as improved compatibility with various polymers and better dispersibility. This modification allows it to serve specialized applications in industries such as lubricants, paints, and sealants, catering to diverse performance needs.</p></p>
