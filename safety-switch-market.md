<p><p>The safety switch market is divided into two main types: contact switches and non-contact switches. Contact switches operate by physically engaging with a mechanism to ensure safety, commonly used in industrial applications for machinery protection. Non-contact switches, on the other hand, utilize technologies like magnetic or infrared sensing for safety applications, allowing for a safer and more flexible operation without physical contact. Both types are essential for safeguarding personnel and equipment in various environments, enhancing operational safety.</p></p>
