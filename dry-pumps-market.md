<p><p>The dry pumps market comprises various types, including claw dry pumps, oil-less rotary vane dry pumps, rotary screw dry pumps, and others. Claw dry pumps utilize twin claw rotors for efficient gas transport without lubrication. Oil-less rotary vane dry pumps leverage vanes to create a vacuum without oil contamination. Rotary screw dry pumps employ intermeshing screws for continuous gas extraction, providing high efficiency. Other pump types may include scroll or diaphragm models, catering to specialized applications across diverse industries.</p></p>
