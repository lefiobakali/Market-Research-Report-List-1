<p><p>The woodworking planers market is categorized into three main types: manual planers, electric planers, and stationary planers. Manual planers are handheld tools used for fine finishing and adjusting wood thickness, suitable for small projects. Electric planers offer greater efficiency and ease of use for larger tasks, powered by batteries or mains electricity. Stationary planers are larger machines positioned in workshops, designed for heavy-duty work, providing precision and consistency for thinning and flattening large wood pieces in various woodworking applications.</p></p>
