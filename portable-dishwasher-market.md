<p><p>The portable dishwasher market primarily features two types: spray type dishwashers and ultrasonic type dishwashers. In contrast, ultrasonic type dishwashers employ ultrasonic waves to create high-frequency vibrations that dislodge debris from surfaces. Both types cater to the growing demand for compact and versatile kitchen appliances.</p></p>
