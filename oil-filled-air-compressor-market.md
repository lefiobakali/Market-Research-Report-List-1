<p><p>The oil-filled air compressor market is categorized into three main types: rotary, reciprocating, and centrifugal. Rotary compressors utilize rotating mechanisms to compress air, offering continuous operation and efficiency, ideal for larger applications. Reciprocating compressors use pistons to compress air in cycles, providing high pressure and suitable for smaller tasks. Centrifugal compressors operate using high-speed rotating impellers to create airflow, often employed in large-scale industrial applications due to their ability to maintain high efficiency at significant capacities.</p></p>
