<p><p>The microfluidic device pumps market is categorized into two main types: electronic type microfluidic device pumps and traditional type microfluidic device pumps. Electronic type pumps utilize advanced technologies, such as piezoelectric actuators and electromagnetic systems, to precisely control fluid flow, offering high accuracy and automation for applications in diagnostics and drug delivery. On the other hand, traditional type microfluidic pumps rely on mechanical systems and gravity-driven methods, providing simpler designs suited for educational and low-cost applications. Both types cater to diverse market needs.</p></p>
