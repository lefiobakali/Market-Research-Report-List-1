<p><ul><li>One-Component Primer Filler</li><li>Two-Component Primer Filler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The primer filler market is segmented into one-component and two-component types. One-component primer fillers are ready to use and provide ease of application, making them ideal for quick projects. They offer good adhesion and filling properties. In contrast, two-component primer fillers require mixing a base and hardener, resulting in enhanced durability and chemical resistance. This type is often preferred for industrial applications where robust performance is critical. Both types serve different needs in automotive, construction, and woodworking sectors.</p></p>
