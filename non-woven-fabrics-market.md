<p><strong>The Non-woven Fabrics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry-Laid Type</li><li>Spunmelt Type</li><li>Wet-Laid Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-woven fabrics market includes three primary types: Dry-Laid, Spunmelt, and Wet-Laid. Dry-Laid non-wovens are formed by laying fibers onto a surface, typically bonded through various techniques. Spunmelt non-woven fabrics involve melt-blowing or spunbonding processes, combining thermoplastic fibers for enhanced strength and durability. Wet-Laid non-wovens use a slurry of fibers suspended in water, creating flexible and absorbent materials. Each type serves diverse applications, including medical, hygiene, and industrial uses, driven by their unique characteristics and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
