<p><ul><li>Moving Coil Velocity Sensor</li><li>Piezoelectric Velocity Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The velocity sensors market comprises various types, primarily focusing on moving coil and piezoelectric velocity sensors. Moving coil velocity sensors operate based on electromagnetic principles, providing accurate measurements for low-frequency applications. In contrast, piezoelectric velocity sensors utilize the piezoelectric effect to convert mechanical vibrations into electrical signals, making them suitable for high-frequency applications. Both sensor types play crucial roles in industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace, enabling effective monitoring and control of machinery and structural integrity.</p></p>
