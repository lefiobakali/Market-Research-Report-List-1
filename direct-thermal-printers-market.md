<p><p>Direct thermal printers are devices that utilize heat-sensitive media to produce images or text without the need for ink or ribbons. Instead, they work by applying heat directly to the specially coated thermal paper, causing it to darken in specific areas. This technology is widely used in various applications, including shipping labels, receipts, and barcode printing due to its efficiency and cost-effectiveness.</p><p>The Direct Thermal Printers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand in retail, logistics, and healthcare sectors.
