<p><p>Non-Invasive Prenatal Testing (NIPT) utilizes advanced technologies to screen for chromosomal abnormalities in a fetus through maternal blood samples. The market comprises several key types: Ultrasound uses sound waves to create fetal images, while Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS) analyzes genetic material for mutations. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) amplifies DNA to detect specific genomic changes, and Microarray technology evaluates many genes simultaneously for abnormalities. Each method offers unique benefits, enhancing prenatal care and enabling informed decision-making.</p></p>
