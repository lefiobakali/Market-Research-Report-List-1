<p><p>Percutaneous Transluminal Angioplasty (PTA) balloons are medical devices used to treat vascular occlusions caused by atherosclerosis or other conditions. They are inserted into narrowed blood vessels via a catheter and inflated to compress plaque against the arterial wall, restoring blood flow. Each type serves distinct therapeutic purposes, addressing various clinical needs in treating peripheral and coronary artery diseases.</p></p>
