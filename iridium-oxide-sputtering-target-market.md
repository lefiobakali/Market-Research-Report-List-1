<p><p>The Iridium Oxide Sputtering Target market is primarily categorized into two types: Rotatable Transformation and Non-Rotating Type. Rotatable targets facilitate continuous deposition by allowing the target to spin during the sputtering process, enhancing uniformity and efficiency. In contrast, Non-Rotating Type targets are stationary, relying on fixed positioning which may result in less uniform coatings. Both types serve various applications, including electronics and optics, but differ in their operational dynamics and effectiveness in achieving desired thin film quality.</p></p>
