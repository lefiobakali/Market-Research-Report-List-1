<p><p>The viscosity tester market includes various types, primarily Engler, Sagbolt, and Redwood viscosity testers. Engler viscometers measure the flow time of liquids at specific temperatures, often used in industrial applications. Sagbolt viscometers assess the viscosity of petroleum products by measuring the flow in a calibrated tube, ideal for assessing heavy oils. Redwood viscometers are designed for measuring the viscosity of lighter oils at higher temperatures, providing essential data for refining processes. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
