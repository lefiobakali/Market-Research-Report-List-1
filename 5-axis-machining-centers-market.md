<p><p>5-axis machining centers are classified into two main types: 5-axis horizontal machining centers and 5-axis vertical machining centers. Horizontal centers are designed for milling operations that require stable cutting and enhanced chip removal, making them ideal for larger workpieces. In contrast, vertical centers offer precision and flexibility for intricate parts, allowing for optimal tooling visibility and access. Both types enhance productivity and accuracy in various industries, enabling complex geometries and reducing setup times through simultaneous movement along multiple axes.</p></p>
