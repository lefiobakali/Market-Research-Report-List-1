<p><ul><li>Non-contact Type</li><li>Contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biopotential sensors are classified into two main market types: non-contact and contact type. Non-contact sensors utilize technologies like capacitive sensing or infrared to detect biopotentials without direct skin contact, making them ideal for hygiene-sensitive applications. In contrast, contact type sensors require physical attachment to the skin, typically through electrodes, offering higher sensitivity and accuracy for monitoring bio-signals like ECG and EMG. Both types serve various medical and wellness applications, each with unique advantages catering to specific user needs.</p></p>
