Sludge pumps handle thick, viscous fluids containing solids, making them ideal for wastewater treatment. Slurry pumps transport a mixture of liquid and solid materials, commonly used in mining and agriculture. Other types may include submersible pumps and vacuum pumps, catering to diverse industrial needs for efficient water and waste management.</p></p>
