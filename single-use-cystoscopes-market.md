<p><p>The single-use cystoscope market is divided into two primary types: rigid cystoscopes and flexible cystoscopes. Rigid cystoscopes are designed for direct visualization and are commonly used in surgical settings due to their durability and precise imaging capabilities. In contrast, flexible cystoscopes offer enhanced maneuverability, allowing for imaging in difficult-to-reach areas, making them ideal for outpatient procedures. Both types are gaining traction in the market due to their hygiene benefits and the reduced risk of cross-contamination.</p></p>
