<p><strong>The Mechanical Tyre Vulcanizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AFV</li><li>BOM</li><li>AVBO</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mechanical Tyre Vulcanizer market comprises three main types: AFV (Automatic Floor Vulcanizers), BOM (Batch Operated Machines), and AVBO (Auto Variable Batch Operated) machines. AFV models are designed for high efficiency and automation, streamlining the vulcanization process with minimal manual intervention. BOM machines focus on batch production, ideal for smaller operations that require flexibility in volume. AVBO systems combine features of both, allowing for variable batch sizes and automated processes, catering to diverse production needs in the tyre manufacturing sector.</p></p>
