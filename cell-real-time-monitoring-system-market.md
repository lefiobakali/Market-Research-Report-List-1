<p><p>The Cell Real-Time Monitoring System market encompasses various imaging techniques essential for observing cellular processes. Confocal imaging offers high-resolution, three-dimensional views by using laser light, allowing for precise localization of cellular components. Phase contrast imaging enhances the visibility of transparent specimens without staining, making it ideal for live-cell observations. Fluorescence imaging involves tagging cells with fluorescent markers, enabling the visualization of specific proteins or structures. Other techniques may include brightfield microscopy and time-lapse imaging, broadening the scope of cellular analysis.</p></p>
