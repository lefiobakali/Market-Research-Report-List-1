<p><ul><li>Single Layer Coating</li><li>Multilayer Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AR (anti-reflective) coated ball lenses market is segmented into two main types: single-layer coating and multilayer coating. Single-layer coatings provide a basic level of reflection reduction, enhancing light transmission and image clarity. In contrast, multilayer coatings consist of multiple layers, significantly improving performance by minimizing reflections across a wider range of wavelengths. This results in superior optical performance, making multilayer coatings more suitable for high-precision applications, such as in cameras, microscopes, and other optical devices.</p></p>
