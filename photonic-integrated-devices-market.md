<p><p>The photonic integrated devices market encompasses various components essential for manipulating light in communication systems. Lasers generate coherent light, while modulators encode information onto light signals. Detectors convert optical signals back to electrical form, and attenuators manage signal strength. Multiplexers and de-multiplexers combine or separate different wavelength channels, enhancing data capacity. Optical amplifiers boost signal power over long distances, maintaining signal integrity. Together, these components drive advancements in data transmission, telecommunications, and various optical applications.</p></p>
