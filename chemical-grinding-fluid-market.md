<p><p>The chemical grinding fluid market can be categorized into two main types: mechanical action polishing fluid and chemical action polishing fluid. Mechanical action polishing fluids rely on abrasive particles to enhance surface finish through physical interaction, effectively removing material and smoothing surfaces. In contrast, chemical action polishing fluids utilize chemical reactions to modify surface properties, often enhancing the material's appearance or corrosion resistance. Both types serve essential roles in various industries, including manufacturing and aerospace, ensuring optimal surface quality.</p></p>
