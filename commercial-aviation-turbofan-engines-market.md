<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Commercial Aviation Turbofan Engines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-Bypass Turbofan Engines</li><li>Low-Bypass Turbofan Engines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial aviation turbofan engines market is primarily divided into two categories: high-bypass turbofan engines and low-bypass turbofan engines. High-bypass engines, commonly used in passenger aircraft, feature a larger bypass ratio, enhancing fuel efficiency and reducing noise. They operate by directing a significant amount of airflow around the engine core. In contrast, low-bypass turbofan engines are typically found in military and regional aircraft, prioritizing thrust over efficiency, with a smaller bypass ratio that provides higher speed and performance.</p></p>
