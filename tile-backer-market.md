<p><p>The tile backer market encompasses various types designed for supporting tile installations. Cement backer board is moisture-resistant and ideal for wet environments, providing durability and strength. Gypsum backer board, lighter and easier to cut, offers a good moisture barrier for dry areas. Foam tile backer boards are lightweight and provide excellent thermal insulation, making them suitable for underfloor heating applications. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to different project requirements in residential and commercial construction.</p></p>
