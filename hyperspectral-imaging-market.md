<p><strong>The Hyperspectral Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Visible/Near Infrared (VNIR)</li><li>Short Wave Infrared (SWIR)</li><li>Medium Wave Infrared (MWIR)</li><li>Long Wave Infrared (LWIR)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hyperspectral imaging market encompasses various types based on wavelength ranges. Visible/Near Infrared (VNIR) typically covers 400-1000 nm, ideal for agriculture and environmental monitoring. Short Wave Infrared (SWIR), ranging from 1000-2500 nm, is used for mineral identification and food quality analysis. Medium Wave Infrared (MWIR) and Long Wave Infrared (LWIR) cover 3-8 µm and 8-15 µm respectively, primarily serving military, surveillance, and thermal imaging applications. The "Others" category includes specialized sensors and emerging technologies for niche applications.</p></p>
