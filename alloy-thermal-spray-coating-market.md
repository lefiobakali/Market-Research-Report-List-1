<p><p>The Alloy Thermal Spray Coating Market comprises various application techniques, notably the combustion flame process and electrical process. The combustion flame process utilizes fuel and oxygen to create a high-temperature flame that melts the alloy powder, allowing it to be sprayed onto surfaces for protective and functional coatings. In contrast, the electrical process, such as arc spraying, employs an electric arc to melt the alloy, enabling precise control and a finer coating finish. Both methods are essential for enhancing surface durability and performance.</p></p>
