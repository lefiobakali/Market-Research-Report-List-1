<p><p>The Semiconductor Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) Equipment market primarily encompasses two types: Thermal Atomic Layer Deposition (TALD) Equipment and Plasma-Enhanced Atomic Layer Deposition (PEALD) Equipment. TALD uses thermal energy to facilitate the deposition process, yielding high-quality films with excellent conformality for various semiconductor applications. In contrast, PEALD leverages plasma to enhance reaction rates at lower temperatures, allowing for better control over film properties and enabling the deposition of complex materials and structures in advanced semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
