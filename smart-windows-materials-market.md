Electrochromic materials adjust their transparency when an electric charge is applied, enabling precise control over light and heat entry. Thermochromic materials react to temperature changes, altering their color based on thermal conditions, thereby enhancing energy efficiency. Together, these technologies improve comfort, aesthetics, and energy management in various applications.</p></p>
