Pneumatically-actuated diaphragm valves utilize compressed air to automate flow regulation, offering quick response times and minimal manual intervention, making them ideal for complex processes. In contrast, manual diaphragm valves operate through manual manipulation, providing simplicity and reliability for less dynamic applications. Both types leverage the corrosion resistance and durability of nickel cobalt alloy, ensuring longevity and performance in demanding environments.</p></p>
