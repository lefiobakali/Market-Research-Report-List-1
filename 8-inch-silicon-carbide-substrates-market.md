<p><strong>The 8-Inch Silicon Carbide Substrates Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conductive Type</li><li>Semi-insulating Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 8-inch silicon carbide substrates market is divided into two primary types: conductive and semi-insulating. Conductive type substrates facilitate efficient electrical conduction, making them ideal for high-power and high-frequency applications. In contrast, semi-insulating substrates exhibit high resistivity, reducing parasitic capacitance and leakage currents, which is beneficial for RF and microwave applications. Both types are crucial for advancing technology in power electronics, automotive, and telecommunications, enhancing device performance, thermal management, and overall reliability.</p></p>
