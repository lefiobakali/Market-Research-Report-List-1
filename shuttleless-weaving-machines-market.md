<p><strong>The Shuttleless Weaving Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Jet Weaving Machine</li><li>Water Jet Weaving Machine</li><li>Rapier and Projectile Weaving Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shuttleless weaving machines market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Air Jet Weaving Machines** utilize high-speed jets of air to propel the weft thread through the warp, allowing for fast production and a wide range of fabrics. </p><p>2. **Water Jet Weaving Machines** employ a high-pressure water jet to insert the weft, ideal for lightweight fabrics but limited by water-sensitive materials. </p><p>3. **Rapier Weaving Machines** use a thin rod to carry the weft thread, providing versatility for various fabric types. </p><p>4. **Projectile Weaving Machines** deploy a small projectile to deliver the weft, suitable for heavy and technical textiles. Each type offers unique advantages tailored to different weaving needs.</p></p>
